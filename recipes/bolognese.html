Bolognese sauce

Description

Bolognese sauce, tomato-based meat sauce for pasta attributed to the Italian city of Bologna.Cooked long and slow, it's rich, hearty, and full of savory flavor

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Meat
  • Tomatoes
  • Carrots
  • Celery

How to make bolognese

  1. Cook Veggies & Meat: Brown the chopped veggies and the meat, then drain off any fat.
  2. Add remaining ingredients and allow it to simmer until thick.
  3. While the sauce simmers, cook pasta according to the directions. Add salt and pepper and parmesan, serve and enjoy!
